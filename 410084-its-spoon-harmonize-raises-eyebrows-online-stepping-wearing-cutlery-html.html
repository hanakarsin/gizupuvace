It's Too Spoon: Harmonize Raises Eyebrows Online after Stepping out Wearing Cutlery

Publish date: 2024-05-15

- Harmonize's outfit bearing spoons, forks, and knives is the talk of the internet

- The singer wore the attire for the music video of Attitude featuring Awilo Longomba

- He has since been trolled by top comedians, among them Mammito and Eric Omondi

Tanzanian singer Harmonize today surprised netizens after sharing images wearing cutlery.

In a series of photos shared on Instagram, the singer wore a black, sleeveless leather jacket with spoons, forks and knives attached to it.

The unorthodox outfit made by Jay Styles fashion was worn by the singer in the music video of his recent hit dubbed Attitude alongside Awilo Longomba.

Even before Harmonize shared still images of the outfit, the dress code had attracted top Kenyan comedians Eric Omondi and Mammito.

In her rendition of the attire, Mammito is seen with an electric kettle, sieve, and cups attached to her clothes.

She playfully poked fun at the singer by implying he could not explain the meaning of the song’s hook.

Eric Omondi also replicated the same and was recorded dancing to the song, which has thus far garnered over 4.7 million views four days after release.

Harmonize shared videos of the two comedians on his timeline, playfully urging President Uhuru Kenyatta to arrest them.
