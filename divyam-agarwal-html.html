Divyam Agarwal - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-10

Entrepreneur, musician and writer best known for his Instagram account i.am.deevs. He's the owner of the digital marketing company Swagger Deevs. He's founded over ten marketing startups. He uses his YouTube channel DivyamAgarwalMusic to promote LoFi music, which he also produces. His music can be found on various platforms and streaming services. He has written books on marketing that can be found on Amazon.

Before Fame

His passion for music and business began when he was a child. He began posting to Instagram in 2021.

Trivia

His 2022 video of himself riding a motorcycle through a mountainside received over 2 million views on Instagram. He has generated a following of 950,000 on Instagram. He has founded more than ten startup companies, including SWAGGER DEEVS, Deevs Mp3, The Real Preneur and InstantPress.

Family Life

He was born and raised in Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh, India. He grew up in a business-oriented family. His parents are named Pankaj and Divya. He has a sibling named Shivam.

Associated With

He and Dev Raiyani are Instagram stars from India.
