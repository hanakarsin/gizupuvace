Don't marry someone who loves you uncontrollably - Pastor advises

Publish date: 2024-05-21

- Popular social media commentator Reno Omokri recently warned men against marrying women who live expensive lifestyles

- According to Omokri, an unemployed lady with N108k hairstyle will end up being a liability

- Omokri also advised men against marrying someone who loves them uncontrollably

Nigerian author and social media commentator Reno Omokri has advised men against marrying some certain kind of ladies.
