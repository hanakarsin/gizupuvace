Rush Furrha - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-15

Social media personality who gained recognition along with his parents and siblings for their The Furrha Family TikTok account. He posts relatable family content and situational humor. They have amassed 12 million fans. 

Before Fame

He posted his first TikTok video in March of 2020. 

Trivia

He has worked as an insurance agent. One of his most popular TikToks features a jewelry store in Chicago. It has gained over 12 million views. 

Family Life

He has eight siblings, including Rocky, Samah, and Kies Furrha. 

Associated With

On November 25, 2021, he posted a Thanksgiving TikTok video set to "Enchanted" by Taylor Swift.
