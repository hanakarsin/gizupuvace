Sean Blakemore - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-25

Famous for his Daytime Emmy-nominated performance as Shawn Butler on the ABC soap opera General Hospital, he is also notable for his appearances on Days of Our Lives, ER, and The Young and the Restless.

Before Fame

Early in his career, he appeared in a television movie titled Playing with Fire.

Trivia

He played a Klingon in the 2013 film Star Trek Into Darkness.

Family Life

He and his brother Ronnell and sisters Rechelle, Sharon, Yvette, Kim, and Yvonne grew up in St. Louis, Missouri. He and Nadyia Jones began their marriage in 2010.

Associated With

While on General Hospital, he shared scenes with Haley Pullos.
