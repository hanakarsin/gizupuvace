Tearful groom reads vows to step-daughter as he marries her mother in heartwarming photos

Publish date: 2024-07-29

Guests attending the wedding ceremony of Jimmy Gisondi and Kelsie Jewert were left with wet eyes after the groom decided to do things a bit different.

Jimmy, a resident of Horsham, Pennsylvania excited his guests and countless people online after reading vows to his 9-year-old step-daughter, Olivia Jewert as he wed her mum on Saturday, November 2.

According to ABC News, the groom had already read his vows to his bride when he turned to the daughter with his heartfelt vows.

While Jimmy's vows to his wife impressed many, it is his promise to the young Olivia that really touched the hearts of thousands.

"Olivia, from this day forward, I promise to remind you every day how truly loved you are. I promise to teach, guide, respect you for the rest of our days. I promise to never treat anything less than my own you child. I am very happy to say that you are my daughter. From this day forward, you will truly be my daughter and the other love of my life. I love you, Olivia." Jimmy vowed.

According to the bride, Kelsie who talked to ABC News days after the wedding, Jimmy's vows or rather gesture meant so much to Olivia.

"It was a very emotional moment for our families it’s something everyone knew Olivia needed and wanted so badly. And that was her time. Olivia is not a very emotional person so seeing her like that just confirmed we all knew how much this just really meant to her." Kelsie said.

The mother of one added that her daughter may have been moved by the vows because before Jimmy, she was raising her all alone as a single mother.

Kelsie revealed she met her hubby Jimmy in 2017.
