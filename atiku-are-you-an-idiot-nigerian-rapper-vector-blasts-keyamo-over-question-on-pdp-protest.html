Atiku: Are You An Idiot?

Publish date: 2024-05-30

Nigerian rapper, Olanrewaju Ogunmefun David popularly known as Vector has slammed the Minister of State for Labour and Employment, Festus Keyamo over his comment on the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) protest.

He reacted to a post by Keyamo, who is also a spokesman of the All Progressives Congress Presidential Campaign Council, where he demanded to know why the opposition was embarking on a protest challenging the results of the presidential election.

Naija News reports that PDP presidential candidate Atiku Abubakar on Monday March 6, joined PDP supporters to demonstrate at INEC’s headquarters as they rejected the declaration of Bola Tinubu as president-elect.

PDP leaders invited to the protest match include the Chairman of PCC and Governor of Akwa Ibom State, Udom Emmanuel; the DG of PCC and Sokoto State Governor, Aminu Tambuwal.

Governors of Bayelsa, Edo, Adamawa, Bauchi, Taraba and Osun States; Former Senate Presidents, Senator David Mark and Senator Dr Abubakar Bukola Saraki, BOT members, NEC members, PDP Senators and House of Representatives Members, NWC members of the party, DDGs, Directors, Deputies and Assistant Directors of NCMC;

Members of the PCC; Leadership of People Living With Disabilities and all other Stakeholders.”

Reacting, Keyamo wondered what the reason behind protest is.

He wrote; “I understand PDP is planning a protest. Question: are they also protesting that LP rigged in the areas where LP won? Are they saying @officialABAT should not have won the South West? Are they calling for cancellation of areas they won in the North? What really are they saying?.”

In response to Kenyamo’s Tweet, Vector wrote; “One more question? Are you an idiot?”
