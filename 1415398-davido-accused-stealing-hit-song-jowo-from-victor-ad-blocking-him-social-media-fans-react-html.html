Singer Victor AD Finally Reacts to Claims That Davido Stole Hit Song Jowo and Blocked Him on Social

Publish date: 2024-05-17

- Top Nigerian singer, Davido, has been embroiled in another controversy on social media after claims that music star, Victor AD, accused him of theft

- According to a Twitter user, Thug Life, Victor reportedly claimed that the 30BG star stole his hit track ‘Jowo’ from him and proceeded to block him on social media

- The news made the rounds online and internet users had interesting things to say about it

-Victor AD finally reacted to the claims and labelled them as false

Top Nigerian singer, Davido, is undoubtedly not a stranger to controversy. News recently made the rounds on social media that another music star, Victor AD, claimed that the DMW boss stole his hit track, Jowo, from him.

In a post shared on Twitter by a user called Thug Life, he claimed that Victor AD reportedly confirmed to Hip TV during an interview that Davido stole Jowo.

According to the post, the 30BG star heard the track when they both recorded the song they worked on together called Tire You.

The tweet explained further that Victor did not suspect any foul play and was surprised to hear Jowo on Davido’s A Better Time album without his management’s knowledge or consent.
