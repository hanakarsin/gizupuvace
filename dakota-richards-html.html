Dakota Blue Richards - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-04

British actress best known for playing the lead role of Lyra in The Golden Compass. She also starred in the films Dustbin Baby and The Secret of Moonacre.

Before Fame

She attended Brighton college.

Trivia

She was on the UK drama Skins from 2011 to 2012.

Family Life

She enjoyed making modern and abstract art during her childhood. She has a half-sister named Maya and a pet dog, Oscar.

Associated With

She was in the fantasy film The Secret of Moonacre with Tim Curry.
