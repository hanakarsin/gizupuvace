Genevieve Nnaji In Steamy Bathroom Scene

Publish date: 2024-03-19

Genevieve Nnaji’s movie, Road To Yesterday, looks about ready to take fans and critics by storm, based on the latest stills currently circulating in the media. 

The actress and her co-star and male lead, Oris Erheuro, were pictured in a steamy bathroom scene.

Erheuro, a British actor and model of Nigerian descent, plays Izu in the movie which also stars Majid Michel, Ebere Okaro and Chioma ‘Chigul’ Omeruah. It was filmed in Lagos and directed by Ishaya Bako.
