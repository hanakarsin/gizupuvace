Funny Whatsapp videos in Kenya

Kenyans love humour, they can create funny stuff even under the most serious circumstances. They develop memes, slogans, videos or even pictures just for fun. Nothing becomes too serious with Kenyans. You will find that these funny events are mostly created and shared on social media platforms, WhatsApp being one of them. Kenyans have benefited a lot from the numerous groups and chats that they have been created from the time the app was introduced in Kenya. If you happen to be in a Kenyan WhatsApp group, you will surely have a good laugh at the way people interact in those groups. While there are some business groups that are kind of serious, most Kenyan WhatsApp groups are for fun.

You will find that there will be a rampant posting of funny details about the everyday life of a Kenyan. Some of the odd information may include a funny WhatsApp video or even a meme. The funny thing about these memes or clips is that they will pop up the minute something has happened in the country, and they will go viral in a blink of an eye because Kenyans love to share funny stuff.

This is a WhatsApp video that went viral and stayed in the minds of most Kenyans for quite a long time.
