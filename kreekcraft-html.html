KreekCraft - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-09

Minecraft and Roblox expert who uploads gameplay videos on his KreekCraft channel, which has earned more than 4.1 million subscribers. He often live-streams his video gameplay on YouTube. 

Before Fame

He launched his YouTube channel in April of 2014 with the username DaKreekCraft. His first video was "Minecraft BOXES Mod - Cardboard Boxes & UPS! (Minecraft v1.6.4 Mod Spotlight)."

Trivia

He earned over 4 million views on a November 2020 YouTube video that featured a replay of a Lil Nas X Roblox performance of "Old Town Road." In 2020, YouTube named him as one of 10 breakout gaming creators on its platform.  

Family Life

His real first name is Forrest and he is from Florida. He was born in Jacksonville, but grew up as an only child in Macclenny. He posted a video in June of 2016 called "My Mother Plays Five Nights at Freddy's 4!" He has a wife named Kayla.

Associated With

In his video "Minecraft: RUMBLE GAMES - Hunger Games Survival," his Minecraft character is killed by CaptainSparklez.
