Jack Wheeler - Trivia, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-03

Entrepreneur and business man who was a White House aid, an official of the Securities and Exchange Commission, CEO of Mothers Against Drunk Driving, and senior planner for Amtrak. On December 31, 2010 his body was found by a landfill worker, and his cause of death was ruled as blunt force trauma. His murder case was never solved. 

Before Fame

He graduated from the United States Military Academy in 1966. 

Trivia

His murder case was featured on an episode of the Netflix series Unsolved Mysteries. 

Family Life

He is a descendant of Military leader Joseph Wheeler. He was born in Laredo, Texas to parents Janet and Joseph. He has a brother named Robert and a sister named Janet. His full name is John Parsons Wheeler III. He was married twice. 

Associated With

He was a presidential aide to George W. Bush and Ronald Reagan.
