Kenneth Pabon - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-04

TikTok sensation known for posting dance, challenge and advice videos. His account has gained over 500,000 followers and more than 22 million likes.

Before Fame

His first TikTok was a post from December 2019 where he walked his puppy, gaining over 400,000 views. In June 2020, he posted a video of himself trying to get his parents to twerk, which gained over 1 million views and more than 100,000 likes.

Trivia

He started a YouTube channel where he posted his first video in August 2020. His channel's content includes vlogs, storytimes and challenges.

Family Life

He often posts TikToks and YouTube videos featuring his family members, including his mother Patty, his father Jerry and his two brothers Ryan and Kelvin. He also has a pet dog named Naomi.

Associated With

He sometimes posts TikToks duetting videos from other creators, including Ondreaz Lopez and Kennie Tatis.
