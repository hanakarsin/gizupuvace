Teachers training colleges provide training for aspiring primary school teachers. Here are some of the teachers training colleges in Kenya.

1. Kagumo Teachers' Training College

Kagumo Teachers Training college is one of the oldest and best TTC college in Kenya. The college is located in Nyeri. The college began as a primary school in the 1930’s but later developed to a teachers training college where it admitted its first 8 students in 1944. Growth for the college went on and by 1982 the college had 6,661 trained teachers ready to serve the nation.

The college offers teachers education in science and humanity subjects such as chemistry, Physics, English, Music, Kiswahili, Mathematics and much more. 2. Kaimosi Teachers' Training College

Kaimosi Teachers Training College offers PI teacher training for primary school teachers. The institution is located at Kaimosi, Kisumu. It institution began in 1904 as a mission school by Edgar Hole and later developed to a teachers training college due to the high demand of teacher education in the country at the time.

The school offers both diploma and certificate courses for primary education. To enroll for diploma courses one must have attained a C plain or have a P1 teachers training and a D plus for certificate courses.

The college offers teacher education in Mathematics English Kiswahili and other subjects. For more information about Kaimosi Teachers College courses visit their official website.

3. Mwingi West Teachers' Training College

Mwingi West Teachers' Training College was founded in 2009 in Mwingi West constituency, Kitui County. Since then the college has been offering quality education which led it emerging 2nd best teachers training college in the Eastern Region. The college offers both certificate and diploma courses.
