Ryan Shakes - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-03

Found fame on social media under the name itsryanshakes, he is best known as a TikTok personality who has garnered more than 6 million fans. He gained fame for his lip-sync, montage and comedy videos, and previously earned more than 2.4 million views in one week for his #AirfreshenerChallenge video. 

Before Fame

His first ever YouTube channel was created back in 2011 when he was 9 and he started out with comedy content. In 2020 he started a company called Shakeyfam. 

Trivia

He has two other TikTok accounts under the names itzryanshakes and shakeyfam. He can also be found on YouTube, where he makes lip-sync, comedy and vlog videos. He's also amassed over one million views on Flipagram and more than 330,000 followers on his itsryanshakes Instagram account. He posted a photo on Instagram of himself standing in his blue high school graduation gown in front of a matching blue Porsche in May of 2020. 

Family Life

He has two sisters named Rochelle and Zayla, and a brother named Zayne. In 2021 he bought his mother a BMW. His middle name is Wayne.

Associated With

He's good friends with fellow social star Matthew Scarfone and Mrgummyking. He is also friends with Tony Paul. He has hung out with Swae Lee. He received an invitation to appear on Shark Tank in 2021, but declined.
