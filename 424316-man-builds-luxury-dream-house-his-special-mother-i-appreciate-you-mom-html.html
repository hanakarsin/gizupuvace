Man Builds Luxury Dream House for His Special Mother: I Appreciate You, Mom

Publish date: 2024-02-16
  • Snaps of a newly built home have social media buzzing
  • The builder of the two-storey residence is a grateful son who wanted to gift his mom a brand new luxury house
  • Mzansi took to the comments section and shared their reactions to the stellar pics

A local man is showing his mom all the love she deserves, building his No. 1 lady a fancy new home. Snaps of the building have since gone viral on social media and many people are commending the man for showing his parent such deep appreciation.

Heading online, @CHIEF_MN is the man behind the life-long present. It seems the newly built home was also meant to commemorate Woman's Day.

"We are wrapping up Women’s Month in style, mom moved in her new house today. I built this house for you because I appreciate you MOM," he captioned the post.
