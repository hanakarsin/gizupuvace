Who Is Tom Schwartz Dating? Let's Just Say The Mystery Continues

Publish date: 2024-04-05

Is Tom Schwartz dating another co-star from 'Vanderpump Rules?' Here's what we know about his romantic status as Season 10 of the show is underway.

Tom Schwartz from Vanderpump Rules was spotted smooching someone new and his ex-wife Katie Maloney was seeing red. As Season 10 of the Bravo show kicked off, the star locked lips with another cast member who is making headlines for another relationship with a co-star as well.

So who was Tom possibly dating that could cause Katie to turn on her ex, whom she once said she had “deep admiration” for? Here’s what we know about a new romance that bloomed on Vanderpump Rules. And, we'll reveal who Tom is said to be linked to following his on-screen kiss.

Tom Schwartz was rumored to be dating another ‘Vanderpump Rules’ co-star after his divorce.

Although Tom and Katie both previously said that their 2022 divorce was amicable, per People, Katie's opinion of her ex went south when Tom was seen making out with Raquel Leviss.

First, as a quick refresher on the love story of Katie and Tom, the couple actually got together pre-Vanderpump Rules (was there such a time?). It was 2017 when the lovebirds said “I do,” per Us Weekly. In 2019, they were seemingly so into one another that they had a second wedding! But by 2022, the marriage was over, with Tom insinuating on Instagram that the decision to end things was Katie’s.

Even so, don’t think that Katie was thrilled to see her former spouse locking lips with Raquel at Scheana Shay’s wedding to Brock Davies. Ahead of the Season 10 premiere, rumors were already circulating that the co-stars had become more than friends.

However, it seems pretty clear at this point that scandalously, Raquel has been secretly dating Tom Sandoval, who was in a long-term relationship with Ariana Madix. Tom and Ariana are no more, and Raquel is definitely enduring a PR nightmare that has nothing to do with Tom Schwartz. Instead, he has moved on to someone new.

Tom Schwartz is reportedly dating someone who is linked to the cast, but is not a Bravolebrity.

In addition to the ongoing bromance between Tom Schwartz and Tom Sandoval, the former is reportedly coupled up with someone outside of the incestuous circle of castmates on Vanderpump Rules.

According to the Daily Mail, Tom is seeing hairstylist Jo Wenberg, with the two spending time together in Los Angeles. Although Tom previously claimed that he and Jo are just friends, and that she was actually his roommate, the outlet speculates they have moved on to romantic status.

Tom has called Jo "a beautiful soul," and "a really cool human being," so the fact that he reportedly fell for her shouldn't shock many fans. Still, it's important to note that at time of writing, Tom had not confirmed that he is officially dating Jo, and instead, recently declared he is not ready for another relationship after his 12-year romance with Katie ended.

"I'm kind of falling in love with myself all over again," Tom said, while denying that he was more than friends — and at least one-time roommates — with Jo, who is a close friend of Kristen Doute, another Vanderpump Rules star.

Guess we'll have to wait and see if Tom and Jo are indeed friends or more than that as Season 10 of the addictive Bravo show continues to play out for fans.
