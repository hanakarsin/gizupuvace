Abel Mutua's Breathtaking Wife Cuts Off Long Hair, Flaunts New Short Hairstyle

Publish date: 2024-07-13

- Judy Nyawira shared a picture of herself dotting a new short hairstyle

- The gorgeous mum decided to join the team natural by cutting her hair and dyeing it maroon

- Her followers liked the new look, with some saying that she still looked on point with or without a long hair

Actor and content creator Abel Mutua's wife Judy Nyawira stunned fans after rocking a new hairstyle. She decided to cut her long hair and dye it.

Nyawira shared a picture of her cute self on Instagram showcasing her natural maroon hair, and her fans loved it.

This is what the gorgeous mom shared online:

"Loving my new hair! The fact that I can shower head to toe is such freedom!"

Many female celebrities within Kenya and beyond have been rocking short hair, with some attributing the move to cut hair to find their true sense of self-love by looking natural.
