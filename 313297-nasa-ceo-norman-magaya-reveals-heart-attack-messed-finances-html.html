NASA CEO Norman Magaya reveals heart attack messed up his finances

Publish date: 2024-06-28

Combative National Super Alliance (NASA) CEO Norman Magaya has revealed how battling a heart attack has been costly and devastating.

Magaya, who has been out of public limelight since the beginning of the year, disclosed in May 2019 that he had suffered a heart attack, exuding confidence he would recover.

The youthful politician on Monday, August 19, opened up on how the dreaded condition has "messed up" his finances and completely changed his lifestyle.

Magaya noted even his medical cover had been reduced into a "joke" and was of little help in the quest to overcome the ailment.

"Nothing puts your life on a freeze like a heart attack ! Complete lifestyle change.Your medical insurance cover becomes a big joke! Your finances get completely messed up. I have learnt tough lessons. But hey I'm a son of God ! I will overcome," he tweeted

The youthful lawyer would be remembered for his combative and aggressive approach in defending and advancing NASA agenda in the run up to 2017 General Election.

He was part of the team that vowed and made good its promise of swearing in Raila Odinga as the president after controversial election results.

Following his revelation that the condition had messed up his life, netizens trooped to his page to wish him quick recovery and share messages of encouragement.
