Corinne Leigh - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-16

YouTube personality best known for being part of the ThreadBanger YouTube channel that has racked up more than 3.7 million subscribers. She has a personal, self-titled YouTube channel that has more than one million subscribers. The ThreadBanger channel specializes in fashion DIY videos.

Before Fame

She got started on YouTube and social media in 2007. The first video on the ThreadBanger channel was "LA Graffiti Artist Interview / 'Spray Can Michaelangelo.'"

Trivia

Some of the more popular DIY videos on the ThreadBanger channel include "DIY Galaxy Slime - Man Vs. Pin - Pinterest Test #40," "How to make Cleaning Slime - Man Vs. Pin #16," and "DIY Water Blob - Man Vs. Pin #25."

Family Life

She grew up on an Air Force base in New Jersey. She lost her mother to cancer. She and her husband Rob Czar, whom she runs ThreadBanger with, began dating around 2004.

Associated With

She and Rob collaborated with Kevin Lieber on a video called "DIY Edible Water Bottles - Man Vs. Science #1 (w/Vsauce2)."
