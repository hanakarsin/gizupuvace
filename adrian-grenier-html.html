Adrian Grenier - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-21

Actor who played the role of Vincent Chase on the award winning television show, Entourage and can be seen in the films, Drive Me Crazy and The Devil Wears Prada.

Before Fame

He was considered to be a hippie bongo drum player during his time in college.

Trivia

In 2007, People magazine ranked him #7 in its annual list of Sexiest Men Alive.

Family Life

His parents are Karesse Grenier and John T. Dunbar. He married Jordan Roemmele in 2022. They welcomed their first child together, Seiko Aurelius, in June 2023.

Associated With

He was joined by Jeremy Piven, Kevin Connolly, Kevin Dillon and Jerry Ferrara in Entourage.
