A few things about the Vikings TV show

The Vikings is a historical TV series which is being filmed in Ireland. Michael Hirst has both created and written the script of the historical drama. The Vikings TV series shows the emergence of the Vikings. It also shows Ragnar Lothbrok’s journey. He was the 1st Viking legend to emerge from the Norse community and finally finds himself on the sheets of History. Ragnar was the mighty warrior. This TV series tells the story of Ragnar, his brother and the Vikings. Ragnar is considered to be a direct successor of Odin, the God of wars and warriors. This series shows the rise of Ragnar from an Earl to a King. After the exit of Ragnar Lothbrok, the show focuses on his sons and their journey as the Viking warriors. The TV series shows the struggle of Ragnar along with his successful invasion of England. He was finally crowned as the king of the Vikings.
