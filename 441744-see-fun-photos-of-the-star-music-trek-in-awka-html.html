See Fun Photos Of The Star Music Trek In Awka

Publish date: 2024-08-07

The city of Awka played perfect host as the 3rd leg of the Star Music Trek berthed with its A-list musical acts.

The 3 leg of the 2015 Star Music Trek sponsored by Star Lager held last weekend at De Geogold Hotels.

It featured show stopping performances from 2face Idibia, Timaya, Yemi Alade, MI, Do2dtun, Naeto C, Raw Nwanne, Joe El, DJ Snoop Damaja, DJ Lambo, DJ Switch and a host of others.

It was in this same musical fiesta that a female fan was seen crying uncontrollably as MI Abaga took to the stage to perform.

We wonder what emotional attachment the adorable fan had with MI's lyrics, songs and charisma.
