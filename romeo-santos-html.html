Romeo Santos - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-18

Bachata singer-songwriter who is the lead singer of the band Aventura. His 2013 hit "Propuesta Indecente" and his 2018 duet with Ozuna, "El Farsante," have each surpassed 1 billion views on YouTube. As a solo artist, he's released albums like Formula, Vol. 1 and Vol. 2, Golden and Utopía. 

Before Fame

He was a member of his church choir when he was 12 years old in which he sang both English and Spanish. 

Trivia

He has won numerous awards throughout his career including a 2012 Billboard Music Award for the Top Latin Album. Aventura's song "Obsesion" was number one in Italy for 16 consecutive weeks in 2002. 

Family Life

He was born to a Dominican father and Puerto Rican mother in the Bronx. He has been in a longterm relationship with Francelys Infante. He has two sons. 

Associated With

In 2018, he was featured in the remix of the song "Ella Quiere Beber" by Anuel AA.
