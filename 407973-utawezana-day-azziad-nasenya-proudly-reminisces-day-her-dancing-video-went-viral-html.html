Utawezana fame

After the beauty became an online sensation, there was a debate on whether she was the one who made the Utawezana song by Femi One featuring Mejja famous.

Some fans even said she deserved to be paid for making the song popular as her video was one of the main reason it became a 'national anthem'.

However, Femi One came out to note otherwise, saying the song was already a big hit even before Azziad jumped on it.

The actress also revealed that someone leaked her number leading to calls and messages from thousands of fans.

In a phone interview with TUKO.co.ke, the pretty babe disclosed she had to change her mobile number after receiving 17,000 messages from strangers in days.

Azziad is now a household name and the beauty continues to share videos of her shaking her enviable waist while helping musicians promote their videos.

Speaking of promotions, the beauty's rate card also left fans stunned online by how prominent she had become.

According to the rate card, which came out about eight months ago, a person or company would have to pay KSh 100,000 for a single post on Facebook, Tik Tok and Instagram and about KSh 50,000 for Twitter posts.
