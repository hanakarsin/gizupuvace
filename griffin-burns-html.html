Griffin Burns - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-26

Voice actor known for his work on the anime Jojo's Bizarre Adventure. He is popular on Twitter where he has 200,000 followers. He also appeared in the 2021 film The Addams Family 2. 

Before Fame

He began his voiceover career in 2010 on the video game Vindictus.

Trivia

He is a musician and makes up half of the band New New Girlfriend. His mygriffinburns TikTok has earned a wide following of over 700,000 followers, and in April 2022 he produced a duet with fellow voice-actor Corina Boettger. He has done voices for over 100 different video games, TV series and animated movies throughout the course of career. 

Family Life

He lives in Los Angeles, California. He posted pictures of his mother and father on his Instagram. He has a sister named Antonia.

Associated With

His band opened for the artist Maty Noyes.
