Marzia Kjellberg - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-19

Italian social star and entrepreneur who first gained fame as a YouTube vlogger, running the channel Marzia, previously titled Cutiepiemarzia. She posted fashion videos on YouTube and helped her viewers with speaking Italian. She is extremely popular on Instagram with over 8 million followers on her itsmarziapie account. 

Before Fame

She was a fan of PewDiePie on YouTube, so she sent him a note, and they developed a close relationship in 2010.

Trivia

She founded the pottery and jewellery line Maì and co-founded the clothing and accessories brand Tsuki. 

Family Life

Her maiden name is Bisognin. She was born to Franca and Marziano Bisognin and she has a brother named Davide. She began dating PewDiePie in 2011; they became engaged on April 27, 2018 and they married in August 2019. She and PewDiePie had pugs named Maya and Edgar. They welcomed their first child, Björn, in July 2023. 

Associated With

In July 2019, she posted a picture to Instagram alongside PewDiePie, PJ Liguori and Sophie Newton.
