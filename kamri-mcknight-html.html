Kamri Noel McKnight - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-05

Younger sister of twins Brooklyn and Bailey McKnight who has gone on to amass her own audience on her self-titled YouTube channel. She has earned over 2 million subscribers through her challenge and vlog-style videos.  

Before Fame

Her mother was already making creative hairstyles for her sisters before she was born. She started out appearing on her YouTube channel Cute Girls Hairstyles. 

Trivia

One of her most popular videos, "The PANCAKE ART Challenge," has earned over 4 million views. 

Family Life

She also has sisters named Rylan and Paisley and a brother named Daxton. Her father is Shaun and her famous mother is Mindy McKnight.  

Associated With

Fellow YouTuber Ann Le has collaborated with Cute Girls Hairstyles.
