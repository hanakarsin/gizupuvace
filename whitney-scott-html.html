Stevie Laine Scott - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-24

Best recognized as the adopted child of rapper Eminem. They are the biological child of his ex-wife Kim Scott.

Before Fame

They were born between Eminem and Kim Scott's first divorce when Scott had a relationship with Eric Hartter.

Trivia

Despite denouncing remarriage in a 2002 issue of Rolling Stone, Eminem remarried their mother in 2006.

Family Life

They have two sisters, Hailie and Alaina. Alaina is the biological daughter of their aunt, Dawn Scott.

Associated With

Their uncle, Nate Kane, is a rapper like their father.
