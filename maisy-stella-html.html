Maisy Stella - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-04

Talented actress and singer who makes up one half of the sister music duo known as Lennon & Maisy along with her sister Lennon Stella. She stars as Daphne Conrad in the ABC smash hit Nashville. 

Before Fame

She was interested in acting in addition to music growing up, appearing in both commercials and music videos from a young age. 

Trivia

With her sister, she performed a cover of Robyn's "Call Your Girlfriend" in order to increase publicity so she and Lennon could apply for work visas.

Family Life

She was born in Oshawa, Ontario and later moved to Nashville, Tennessee. Her parents are Brad and Marylynne Stella.

Associated With

She shares the screen with Connie Britton on Nashville.
