Intak - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-19

Rapper and dancer best recognized as a member of the K-pop group P1Harmony. He was the fourth member of the six selected for the band. 

Before Fame

He auditioned for the P1Harmony with the Justin Bieber song "Love Yourself." 

Trivia

 He has appeared on the show I've Fallen For You. His group has produced three studio albums. The videos on their self-titled YouTube channel have gone on to earn over 37 million views.

Family Life

He was born in Yangju, South Korea and moved to New Zealand as a child. 

Associated With

One of his favorite movies is Forrest Gump starring Tom Hanks.
