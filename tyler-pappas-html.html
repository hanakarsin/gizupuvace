Logdotzip - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-06

Named Tyler Pappas, but better known by his YouTube and social media name Logdotzip, he is a Minecraft gamer who plays Minecraft mini-games. He has earned over 4.7 million subscribers to his channel. He has also earned over 150,000 followers on his Instagram page.

Before Fame

When he joined the YouTube world, his channel was intended to be a RuneScape channel. 

Trivia

He was suspended from YouTube due to allegations of spam which were not true. His channel returned after a three-day hiatus. At the end of 2021, he posted a photo on Instagram detailing the twelve books he read that year. 

Family Life

He has a younger sister who has been featured on his Instagram. He has lived in the Team Crafted house in Los Angeles even though he was never a member of that team.

Associated With

He plays Minecraft mini-games with SkyDoesMinecraft.
