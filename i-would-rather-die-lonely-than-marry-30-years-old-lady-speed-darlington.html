'I Would Rather Die Lonely Than Marry 30 Years Old Lady'

Publish date: 2024-02-28

US-based Nigerian hip-hop singer, Darlington Okoye, popularly known as Speed Darlington, has vowed never to marry a lady who is 30 years old or above.

Naija News reports that the controversial rapper stated this in a viral video online, stressing he would rather die lonely than marry any woman in her 30s.

Speed Darlington noted that he prefers to donate all his properties to charity rather than start a family with a 30-year-old woman.

Giving reasons for his decision, the singer claimed that older women always fail to assist their spouses in paying bills despite being financially wealthy.

He added that it is only in Nigeria that men are desperate for rich women, unlike America and Europe.

He said, “I would rather die lonely than marry a 30-year-old woman. I prefer to sell all my things [properties] give it to charity than start a family with a woman in her 30s then keep enough money for the coffin.

“Most men in Africa do not care about rich woman, it is only in America and Europe that rich woman has levels. Sometimes in America, many guys don’t care about a rich woman because the woman will always claim her money is for her. She no dey bring her money for the family only the man dey pay bills.”
