SO SAD: Oshadipe Twins' Taiwo Is Dead

Publish date: 2024-08-10

One of the singing duo of the Oshadipe Twins is dead.

Linda Ikeji reports that the Taiwo of the Oshadipe Twins passed away early hours of today, October 3rd in Lagos. Although full reports about what caused her death are still sketchy as the cause of her death has not been made public.

Taiwo who was married and recently gave birth to her first child last updated her Facebook page on October 1st. The sad incident is sad to have affected her twin who is presently mourning her sister's death.

It would be recalled that just few days ago, a popular Nigerian dancer died 20 days after her wedding, during childbirth.
