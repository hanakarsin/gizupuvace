Pauletta Washington - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-10

She played Terri Angelou on the TV comedy series The Parkers and achieved massive fame upon marrying Denzel Washington.

Before Fame

She briefly appeared in the film Beloved, which was based off a book written by Toni Morrison.

Trivia

She gave birth to twins when she was 39 years old.

Family Life

She married actor Denzel Washington on June 25, 1983. They have four children named John, Olivia, Malcolm and Katia.

Associated With

She has appeared as a guest on The Wendy Williams Show.
