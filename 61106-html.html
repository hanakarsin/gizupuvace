Women In Lagos Protest The Killing Of Children (PHOTOS)

Publish date: 2024-02-20

Some women in Lagos this morning, March 6th took to the street to protest the killings and abduction of Innocent Children in the Northern states.

The women started their protest from the premises of Lagos Television (LTV 8)  and ended at the Lagos State Government Secretariat in Alausa where the Head of Service of Lagos State addressed them.

The event which was brought together by Laila St Matthews had the likes of Activist/President of Women Arise, an NGO, Dr Joe Odumakin, Mrs Bridget Elesin and other women leaders join the protest.

Dr Joe Okei-Odumakin who led the women submitted a letter of protest for onward transmission to the Federal Government requesting the FG to take on the child killers.
