'Thor' Cast Surprises Moviegoers With Live-Action Play Of The Film

By Mustafa Gatollari

Updated Oct. 23 2018, 1:10 a.m. ET

There's a special kind of joy to be had when watching low budget renditions of famous blockbuster films.

Like those super cheap versions of Terminator 2: Judgment Day that are scattered around the internet.

Classic. It's just always funny to see someones low-budget attempt at some of our favorites.

As it turns out though, low budget versions of movies aren't just for fans. No, the stars who acted the real versions of the films themselves can have tons of fun parodying their own roles. Cause hey, it gets boring being rich and famous sometimes.

As proven by this hilarious impromptu live play version of Thor: Ragnarok staged by Chris Hemsworth, Tom Hiddleston, Tessa Thompson, Cate Blanchett, Mark Ruffalo, and Jeff Goldblum. Yes you read that right -- basically the whole cast was in on this ingenious work of art. 

As part of a hilarious Thor: Ragnarok 4D sketch conceived by James Corden and his crew, the stars put on a live performance of the movie for unsuspecting moviegoers going to see the latest Marvel blockbuster. They had no idea, but I'm sure it beats seeing the real thing cause now they have an awesome story to tell.
