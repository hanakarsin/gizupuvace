Graduate standardization exam is the system which will eventually replace all aptitude tests in Nigeria. It`s very similar to General Method Aptitude Test, which originated from the UK. Employers and many other test companies base the structure of their test on this format. It helps to evaluate the capabilities of the future employee. This test format is very simple and consists of three parts:

  • Numerical Reasoning;
  • Verbal Reasoning;
  • Abstract Reasoning.
