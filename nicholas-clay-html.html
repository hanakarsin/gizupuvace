Nicholas Clay - Trivia, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-09

Best known for playing Lancelot in Excalibur and for his role of Alan in The Picture of Dorian Gray.

Before Fame

He began his acting career at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art which led to several theater performances including a role on Broadway.

Trivia

He has appeared in two Sherlock Holmes adaptations, The Hound of the Baskervilles and an episode of The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes.

Family Life

He and his wife, Lorna Heilbron, had two daughters by the names of Ella and Madge.

Associated With

He had a part in the 1978 drama series Will Shakespeare, which starred Tim Curry in the title role.
