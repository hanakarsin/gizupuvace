Naomi Osaka - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-28

Tennis player who captured her first grand slam victory after defeating Serena Williams in the 2018 US Open Championship. She also went on to win the 2019 Australian Open and the 2020 US Open.

Before Fame

She registered to the Japan Tennis Association and then turned pro in September 2013.

Trivia

She won the Rising Stars Invitational exhibition tournament at the 2015 WTA Finals.

Family Life

Her father is Leonard Francois and her sister is Mari Osaka. She and rapper Cordae started dating in 2019.

Associated With

She defeated Madison Keys in the semi-finals of the 2018 US Open.
