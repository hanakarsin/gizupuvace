MeiMei Kuo - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Singer and dancer who originally rose to fame as a member of the Taiwanese girl group Hey Girl. She has appeared on shows like Brown Sugar Macchiato.

Before Fame

She attended the Taipei National University Of The Arts.

Trivia

She has endorsed products like Maybelline and Edwin Jeans.

Family Life

She is the younger sister of model JieJie. Her real name is Kuo Chieh-Chi.

Associated With

He was bandmates with Gui Gui in Hey Girl.
