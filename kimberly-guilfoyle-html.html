Kimberly Guilfoyle - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-06

Victoria's Secret model who also worked as a co-host on the series The Five on Fox News Channel. She has also regularly appeared on programs like Red Eye with Greg Gutfeld and the O'Reily Factor.

Before Fame

She studied law and worked as an Assistant District Attorney before appearing on TV.

Trivia

She once modeled Bridalware for Macy's.

Family Life

She has been married to both Gavin Newsom and Eric Villency. In 2018, she became romantically linked to Donald Trump Jr. She has a son named Ronan.

Associated With

She was a co-host for The Five, which has had Bill O'Reilly as a guest.
