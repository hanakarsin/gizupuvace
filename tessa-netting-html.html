Tessa Netting - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-06

YouTuber, actress, singer, and host also known as tessaROXX, who has appeared on Broadway and various television shows. She's known for her role as Hazel on Disney's Bunk'd. She has over 750,000 subscribers on her YouTube channel. Some of her most popular videos have included content from the Harry Potter franchise. 

Before Fame

She was constantly singing show tunes and dancing at her mother's dance studio, Dance One Studios. 

Trivia

At the age 17, she was in the original Broadway cast of Billy Elliot the Musical and performed in over 1,000 shows. She has posted multiple photos of herself dressed up as characters from Harry Potter on her Instagram account. 

Family Life

She was born to Leslie and David Netting. She also has a sister named Kelsey who is a dancer. She got engaged to actor Joe Moses in April 2019 after dating for seven years. The couple married in March 2022. 

Associated With

She is a big fan of the Starkid theatre company, which has included performers such as Darren Criss, Lauren Lopez, and Joe Walker.
