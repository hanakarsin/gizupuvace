Lucas Jagger - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-19

The seventh child of rock legend Mick Jagger and the first child of Brazilian model Luciana Gimenez. He is extremely popular on social media with over 390,000 followers on Instagram. 

Before Fame

His parents met and became pregnant with him while Mick Jagger was still married to Jerry Hall.

Trivia

To celebrate his 21st birthday in 2020, he posted an Instagram picture that showed him on the cover of a fake magazine titled Not A Teen Magazine. 

Family Life

He has six older half siblings through his father and one younger half brother, Lorenzo Carvalho, born from his mother and stepfather Marcelo de Carvalho.

Associated With

Some of his famous siblings include Elizabeth, Karis, Jade, and Georgia Jagger.
